The beginning

Around in 2010

When I was very young, my mom bought this pink ASUS laptop for me and my brother. I grew up in a very remote area where internet connection was very limited, let alone interest in technology. I was your typical village boy. While everyone were already using the latest iPhone at the time, I was still stuck with a brick phone, playing old school games like Snake, Tetris, Space Invaders and some that I don't even remember. That pink laptop, the first time I opened it opened a lot of possibilities. The thought of internet at the time was very interesting for me. I thought that was cool.

High School

Around in 2015

Around in high school, I became that IT go to guy by myclassmates. I didn't know much about technology at the time, but I was always interested everything in IT. Around in early in high school at around Year 8, I had one of this subjects called Computer something where we learned basic HTML, CSS and Javascript. I didn't enjoyed it too much and thus didn't learned much, maybe the teacher didn't taught us properly that I never understood it. I continued and finished my high school without realising my passion for technology.

After high school

January 2021

Right after high school, I enrolled in TAFE courses to find out more about IT and my future in general. I knew this industry would be hard, but I also knew there's a lot of branches for me to explore on this industry. One of the subjects of that course I took had Python Programming. The teacher was very good at teaching and I enjoyed his class. The first time I wrote print("Hello World") in Python in Week 1 felt the same as I remembered opening that pink laptop when I was little and thus I decided to study Computer Science because I know that's what I want to do.
